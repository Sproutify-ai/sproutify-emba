Assignment Instructions

In this assignment, you will need to evaluate 10 solutions submitted for MIT Solve's 2024 Global Health Equity Challenge. The estimated completion time is 25 - 35 minutes. 2-3 minutes per solution is recommended. You will use two different versions of solution assessment tools, with the first 5 questions using one version and the remaining 5 questions using another version.

Before beginning the assessment, please follow the provided hyperlink to learn details about the challenge and its objectives.

2024 Global Health Equity Challenge

Assessment Process:

  1. For each solution, assess it based on the 5 given criteria.
  2. After completing your assessment, fill in your decision in the upper corner of the assessment page:
    • Decision: Select either "Pass" or "Fail."
    • Fail Reason (if applicable): If the solution fails, select the most obvious criterion that it fails to meet.
    • Confidence Level: Indicate your confidence level in making the decision on a scale of 1 (low) to 5 (high).
  3. Click the "Next" button to move on to the next solution.

Completion:

Upon completing the evaluation of all 10 solutions, please

  1. 1. Take a screenshot of the final web page that shows you have completed your assessment.
  2. 2. Return to Canvas to upload the screenshot of your completion page and fill out the post-evaluation survey.

Important:

  • Start assessment only when you complete the practice.
  • Once you start the assessment, you cannot interrupt the assessment or return to the previous page using your browser's navigation buttons.
  • Please ensure you have sufficient time to complete the assessment in one sitting. This assessment may take you approximately 25 to 35 minutes to complete.
