Research Study Instructions

In this study, you will need to evaluate 14 solutions submitted for MIT Solve's 2024 Global Health Equity Challenge. The estimated completion time is 40 - 60 minutes. 2-3 minutes per solution is recommended. You will use two different versions of solution assessment tools, with the first 7 questions using one version and the remaining 7 questions using another version.

Before beginning the study, please follow the provided hyperlink to learn details about the challenge and its objectives.

2024 Global Health Equity Challenge

Assessment Process:

  1. For each solution, assess it based on the 5 given criteria.
  2. After completing your assessment, fill in your decision in the upper corner of the assessment page:
    • Decision: Select either "Pass" or "Fail."
    • Fail Reason (if applicable): If the solution fails, select the most obvious criterion that it fails to meet.
    • Confidence Level: Indicate your confidence level in making the decision on a scale of 1 (low) to 5 (high).
  3. Click the "Next" button to move on to the next solution.

Completion:

Upon completing the evaluation of all 14 solutions, please fill out a 5-minute post-evaluation survey.
